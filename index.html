Who are we

Sweet Home Farm Development Forum was created not long ago by Mvuleni Luxolo(Chairperson), Zamuxolo Phakamile(Dep Chairperson), Nomthakazelo Mahambehlala(Secretary), Thulani Jabe(Dep Secretary), Phuthumani Vellem(Treasure). It was created due to the lack/slow support from the government in recreational and the well being of the community.
Youth need facilities to showcase and upskill themselves, as we grow as an Organization we will have a project where we will teach people skills courses to reduce the unemployment rate.
Our community is heavily poverty stricken we help where we can. In winter the struggle is real we live in Shacks/Wendy houses, in heavy rains many families suffer due to floods they lose their houses, clothes and food, we try by all means to meet the families halfway but due to the lack of funds and resources not everyone is helped.

Objectives

As Sweet Home Farm Development Forum we want to help the Youth and obviosly older people in recreational and sport.
We currently have Soccer teams, Drama and Dancing clubs, and they lack the necessary resources to properly showcase their skills. e.g. Soccer uniforms, Drums and Recreational facilities.
By helping with what is needed it will surely brings positivity and hope in our community.
